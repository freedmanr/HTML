History

Sports

Sports played a big role in my upbrining. During elementary and middle school, my favorite ones were basketball and sparring. For basketball, I played on the school team, a recreational team, as well on a travel team. As I grew older,however, I started to realize the limited path I was destined for due to my genetics. For sparring on the other hand, genetics were less of an issue and in fact I had quite an accomplished record, winning mutiple touranments, some even international, and was selected to serve as a US representative.

When high school arrived, my interests switched to Football,Rugby,and Shotput. For Rugby, I had to call it quits soon due to being super injury prone. Football on the other hand, consumed most of my free time year round to the end of my junior year. Being in south florida, football was very intense. During the fall season, we had 3 hour practices Monday-Saturday,exclusing Game Days, and in the offseason we would have workouts before and after school. My work ethic was quickly noticed and by my sophmore year I was starting linebacker and junior year I led the team in tackles/sacks. All at the same time, I had the academic schedule of an elite student. I do believe that these years, however painful, gave my the confidence that I can do really hard things you just need to really push yourself to uncomftorable places. I also do belive that in the long run, although I could have been coding or taking additional courses instead, these expiercnes help make me unique from other technical folks.
